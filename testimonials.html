Testimonials

Dr. Baba Musa, PhD

University of Vitez

Director-General, West African Institute for Financial and Economics Management

Having a PhD has always been my aspiration in the sense that, I have always wanted to be part of a research team. My attachment to research is born out of the desire to keep myself engaged in thought provoking activities that break existing barriers and create new frontiers. I am always involved in sharing ideas with people from the academics, the business community and policy makers. With my research background and business leadership background, I have always come as a useful resource to policy makers and am able to bring research and practice together.

As I commenced my PhD journey with Circle International, I realized immediately that I was in the hands of an institution that will expand my horizon and support me to strengthen my academic and business pursuits simultaneously. I noticed the immense challenge I had to face as an independent researcher responsible for developing my own ideas, refining them, documenting them and defending them. However, the significant resources put at my disposal by Circle in terms of library resources, supervisory teams and subject matter experts made the challenges surmountable.

I found the Circle team (academic and administrative) to be very supportive and there was someone at any point in time to provide guidance. Going through literature review in a ‘systematic’ order helped me greatly to organize my thoughts and work according to the relevant themes in my research work. That made the actual proposal writing much easier than I anticipated. Coming out with an acceptable research methodology posed an initial challenge but with, brainstorming with the supervisory team and utilization of some industry insight, I soon came out with a methodology that amazed and excited myself and my supervisory team. In fact, my research methodology was by itself a significant addition to knowledge due it being novel. With a strong and systematically organized literature review, a sound and novel research methodology the stage was set for me to conclude my PhD from the University of Vitez. This was done within two years (submission).
